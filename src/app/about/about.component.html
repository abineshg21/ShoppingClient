About Zapata


   The Zapata Food is the leading Mexican Restaurant in United States. Zapata allows users to conveniently discover food around their neighbourhood and directly order their favourite meals online or via mobile. In the philippines, Zapata has since grown to over resturant nationwide.


   We are a small team of problem solvers, designers, thinkers and tinkers, working around the clock to make foodpanda the most powerful online tool for food delivery. We believe that ordering food should be easy, fast and definitely fun!


    Tried of the same take-out for lunch? Too lazt to go out for dinner? Simply download the Zapata app or go to www.zapata.com, and enter your location to see wich resturants deliver to you. Food delivery has never been easier!

Our Values

Resilience

We push ourselves beyond our abilities when faced with tough times. When we foresee uncertainly, we address it only with flexibility.

Acceptance

Feedback is never taken personally, we break it into positive pieces and strive to work on each and every element event more effectively.

Ownership

People here don't work 'for' Zomoto, they work 'with' Zomoto. We treat every problem as our own, take accountability and drive the change.

Humility

It's always 'us' over 'me'. We don't lose ourselves in pride or confidence during individual successes, but focus on being our simple selves in every which way.

Spark

We belive in, stand for and are evangelists of our culture - both, Within Zomoto and externally with all our stakeholder.

Judgement

It's not our abilities that show who we truly are - it's our choice, We aim to get these right, at least in the majority of the cases.
